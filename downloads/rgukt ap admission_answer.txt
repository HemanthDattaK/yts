## Eligibility Criteria for AP RGUKT IIIT Admissions

The RGUKT IIITs in Andhra Pradesh aim to encourage rural students and provide integrated 6-year B.Tech programs.

### General Eligibility
*   **Educational Qualification:** Candidates must have passed the 10th class (SSC).
*   **Selection Basis:** Admission is primarily based on the merit marks obtained in the 10th class (SSC).
*   **Special Categories:** Reservations for various castes (BC, SC, ST) and special categories like Physically Handicapped (PWD), Children of Armed Personnel (CAP), NCC, Sports, and Scouts are considered.

### Expected 10th Class Scores (for rural students, approximate guidelines)
The second video provides approximate score expectations for admission to the RGUKT IIITs:
*   **Open Category:** 580+ marks
*   **BC Category (BCA, BCB, BCC):** 560+ marks
*   **SC Category:** 520+ marks
*   **ST Category:** 500+ marks

### Course Details
*   The program is an integrated 6-year course comprising:
    *   2 years of Pre-University Course (PUC, equivalent to Intermediate)
    *   4 years of B.Tech Undergraduate Education
*   Admission to the PUC is based on 10th class scores. The B.Tech branch (e.g., Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Electronics - CSE, ECE, EEE, ME, CE) is allotted based on the student's performance in the 2-year PUC program.
*   **Campuses:** The four RGUKT IIIT campuses in Andhra Pradesh are located at Nuzvid, Idupulapaya, Ongole, and Srikakulam (near H.R. referred to as Srikakulam). Nuzvid is considered the headquarter.

### Benefits of RGUKT IIITs
*   Ideal for meritorious students from financially weaker backgrounds who cannot afford fees in other private institutions.
*   Known for strong academic performance, with students often achieving good ranks in the GATE exam for Master's degree admissions.
*   Best suited for students aiming for public sector jobs through GATE scores rather than immediate placements after undergraduate studies.

## Application Process for AP RGUKT IIIT Admissions

The application process is entirely online. The first video provides a step-by-step guide.

### Important Dates (as mentioned in the videos)
*   **Application Period:** May 1st to May 30th (The titles mention "2026" or "2026-27," but the content describes an active application process in "May.")

### Application Website
*   **Official Website:** www.rguket.in
*   The direct link to the application home page is usually provided in the video description and pinned comments.

### Step-by-Step Application Process

1.  **Registration for Fee Payment:**
    *   Visit the official website.
    *   Select the option to "Register for Fee Payment."
    *   Choose "AP Students" if you are from Andhra Pradesh.
    *   Enter your SSC Board (e.g., AP SSC for regular students).
    *   Enter your SSC Hall Ticket Number, Student Name (as per SSC), and Date of Birth (as per SSC).
    *   Select your Reservation Category (Caste).
    *   Provide a Parent's Mobile Number and a working Email ID.
    *   Enter the Captcha code and click "Submit."
    *   A unique **Application Number** will be generated. Note it down.

2.  **Fee Payment:**
    *   Click "Proceed to Payment."
    *   Enter your generated Application Number and click "Get Details."
    *   Verify your details and the application fee displayed.
    *   **Application Fees:**
        *   OC / BC: ₹400
        *   SC / ST: ₹250
    *   Select your preferred Payment Method and click "Proceed to Pay."
    *   Enter card details and complete the payment.
    *   Upon successful payment, an **Application ID** will be generated. Note it down.

3.  **Filling the Online Application Form (7 Stages):**
    *   After successful payment, click "Click here to fill application" then "Click here to apply."
    *   **Stage 1: Personal Details**
        *   SSC Hall Ticket Number, Board, DOB, and Student Name will be auto-filled.
        *   Enter Father's Name, Mother's Name, and select Gender.
        *   Select your Reservation Category (Caste).
        *   **Upload Caste Certificate:** In JPG format, below 100 KB.
        *   **RGUKT Campus Preference:** Select all four AP campuses (Nuzvid, Idupulapaya, Ongole, Srikakulam) in your preferred order. This is mandatory.
        *   **Special Category Reservation:** Indicate if you are Physically Handicapped (PWD), if your parents are in the Army, if you have completed 2 years of NCC (upload certificate if yes), or if you have Sports/Scout certificates.

    *   **Stage 2: Education Particulars**
        *   Enter your total marks obtained in SSC (out of 600).
        *   Enter individual marks obtained in each subject in SSC.
        *   **Details of School Education (4th to 10th Class as per Study Certificate):** For each class from 4th to 10th, provide:
            *   State, District, and Mandal where you studied.
            *   Class-wise School Name.
            *   School Location (Rural/Village).
            *   School Management (Government/Private).
            *   School Type (Private/Government/Model School).

    *   **Stage 3: Other Details**
        *   Enter your Student Aadhaar Number.
        *   Select your Place of Residence (State).
        *   Indicate your Annual Income (Above or Below ₹2.5 Lakh) based on your income certificate.
        *   **Upload Income Certificate:** A fresh Income Certificate (applied after April 1st) in JPG format, below 100 KB.
        *   Select the State mentioned in your Income or Ration Card.

    *   **Stage 4: Communication Details**
        *   Enter a working Parent's Mobile Number.
        *   Enter an Alternative Mobile Number (compulsory).
        *   Enter a Parent's working Email ID.
        *   Enter the Contact Number of your 10th class school.
        *   Enter your complete Correspondence Address (where you currently reside).
        *   If your Permanent Address is the same as your Correspondence Address, check the box.

    *   **Stage 5: Upload Documents**
        *   **Student Passport Photo:** Below 100 KB.
        *   **SSC Hall Ticket:** Below 100 KB.

    *   **Stage 6: Declaration**
        *   Review all the entered information.
        *   Select "Accept" the declaration.
        *   Click "Submit."

4.  **Download Application:**
    *   After successful submission and document uploads, a "Files Uploaded Successfully" pop-up will appear.
    *   Click "Click here to Download" to get a PDF copy of your filled application. Download and save it for future reference.

### Selection Process
*   After the application deadline, candidates will be selected based on their merit marks in SSC, caste category, and special reservations (like sports).
*   Selected candidates will receive a selection message via email/mail.
